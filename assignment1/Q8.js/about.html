About Us

Institute of engineering and techology DAVV, Indore

Institute of engineering and technology DAVV, Indore is one of the best college with AN ISO certified Institution. NAAC ACCREDITED with 'A+' Grade. The college is affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore. The college is located in the heart of the city and is easily accessible from all parts of the city. The college has a sprawling campus with state of the art infrastructure and facilities. The college has a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to the overall development of the students. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines.

The college has a well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, journals, and periodicals. The college has a well-equipped computer lab with high-speed internet connectivity. The college has a spacious auditorium with a seating capacity of 500 people. The college has a well-maintained sports complex with facilities for indoor and outdoor games. The college has a canteen that serves hygienic and nutritious food to the students. The college has a placement cell that provides placement assistance to the students. The college has a well-established alumni network that helps the students in their career growth.
